Unlike traditional treatments, Dermagenesis combines deep tissue repair with surface-level enhancement, leading to long-lasting, natural-looking results.

At Lumicare, we’re driven by a passion for results, safety, and innovation in skincare. Our trusted reputation is built on proven technologies and personalized care that truly transforms skin.

4 modalities, 1 device: Dermagenesis Infusions

DermaPeel Procedure

DermaPeel Procedure (Dry Microdermabrasion) is an entry-level low cost modality that is excellent for clients with oily skin types. The simple handpiece offers disposable. Diamond BP tips in 3 levels of abrasion and 2 shapes - round or oval.

DermaFrac Procedure

DermaFrac Procedure (Micro-Protrusion Exfoliation + Infusion) exfoliates the skin and simultaneously infuses essential serums to the skin. Digital vacuum control technology provides optimal procedures to the client's skin. It enhances the skin tone and texture and reduces fine lines & wrinkles. IT has two MPR tips (0.25 mm & 0.5 mm) to choose from.

DermaBooster Procedure

DermaBooster Procedure (Microdermabrasion + Infusion) is a specialised skin rejuvenation technique that combines the controlled exfoliation of the outer layer of skin and simultaneous serum solutions. It enhances exfoliation & provides additional benefits such as hydration & nourishment with custom serums.

DermaBooster Procedure

DermaJect Procedure (Infuse and smooth) soothes dry skin with intense hydration. It infuses serum under vacuum occlusion with no exfoliation. Vacuum creates an occlusive seal on the skin's surface, enhancing the permeability of the stratum corneum & absorption of specialty serums. It is excellent for sensitive areas such as the eyes, neck & lips.
