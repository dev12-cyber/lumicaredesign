1. ARTIQA Fat & Cellulite Reduction

Thermal Shock targets fatty deposits by applying extreme cold to constrict blood vessels. This non-invasive treatment focuses on adipose tissue to induce lipolysis, reducing body fat without harming the surrounding tissues.

The technique involves alternating warm and cold applications directly on the client's skin.

When exposed to cooling, fat cells undergo apoptosis, a natural and controlled cell death. This triggers the release of cytokines and other inflammatory mediators to gradually eliminate the affected cells. Over the months following the procedure, inflammatory cells systematically digest the targeted fat cells, reducing the thickness of the fat layer. The lipids released from the fat cells are slowly transported through the lymphatic system for processing & elimination.

The first results are generally visible within 2-3 weeks following the first session. Visible results may take longer to appear with thicker layers of fat.

2. ARTIQA Muscle Recovery

Thermal Shock triggers vasodilation and vasoconstriction, increasing the blood flow in the targeted area. It offers instant & long lasting pain relief. Furthermore, it ensures that the functional improvement of joints and the overall well-being of the treated region occur quickly. The cycles of hyperthermic heating, followed by a resting phase, encourage the opening and closing of small blood vessels, enhancing revascularization.

Benefits of Thermal Shock:

  • Anti-inflammatory: Reduces the production of inflammatory mediators.
  • Anti-edema: Helps limit tissue contamination and swelling.
  • Analgesic: Inhibits nerve endings to reduce pain.
  • Muscle Relaxing: Hot-cold alternation relaxes muscle tension.

The suggested treatment frequency is 1× per week for 5–10 sessions. Visible results are noticed after 2–3 sessions.
