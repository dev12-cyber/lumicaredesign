Get to Know About Modula LFP!
  • CE-certified innovative laser diode using Liquid Optical Fiber (LFP) for zero energy loss.
  • Delivers fast, precise, and long-lasting hair removal with no power dispersion.
  • Contact mirror lens with one-way coating ensures minimal refraction and seamless energy transfer.
  • Achieves 95% higher efficiency and reduces treatment time by 25% compared to traditional diode lasers.

The Modula Liquid Fiber Pro marks a breakthrough in hair removal technology, offering a unique blend of efficiency, durability, and precision. Ideal for practitioners aiming to deliver outstanding results with minimal downtime.

Modula LFP is unlike any traditional diode, ever!

Ensures durability, longevity, and an extended lifetime up to 20 years with 100 crore shots—truly one-of-its-kind.

Delivers high treatment efficacy with a flat top beam profile, ensuring minimal divergence and optimal energy distribution.

There are Three Treatment Modes

Stamping Mode

This mode offers high fluence with optimum pulse width for long-lasting results.

Fast Mode

This mode offers low fluence at low pulse width with high repetition rate making treatment fast and comfortable.

Expert Mode

This mode offers high energy at minimum pulse width for better results in fine hair and non-responsive cases.
