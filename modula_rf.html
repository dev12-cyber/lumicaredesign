Get to Know About Modula RF
  • Modula RF is ideal for skin tightening and lifting, enabling collagen remodeling, fine wrinkle reduction, and improved microcirculation through CE-certified technology.
  • Uses Bipolar technology with dual electrodes, allowing superficial (1mm) facial treatment — perfect for sensitive facial skin without reaching deeper dermal layers.
  • Easy transition between Fractional and Bipolar RF modes using the advanced DCS (Detection Control System) for automatic handpiece recognition.
  • Monopolar RF mode enables deeper tissue targeting — treating epidermis, dermis, hypodermis, and even subcutaneous fat — achieving a stronger Tensor Effect.

3-Step Rejuvenation Process to Restore Skin Tone & Elasticity

Monopolar

Deep irradiation for strong Tensor Effect—treats all skin layers from the epidermis to subcutaneous fat using a single electrode.

Bipolar

Targets delicate facial areas with controlled 1mm depth—ideal for superficial facial skin treatment.

Quadripolar

Focuses heat precisely across large areas using 300W output—ensures even distribution without energy loss.

Simple, Practical, User-Friendly – Modula RF

Modula RF facilitates seamless switching between fractional and bipolar RF treatments. Just insert the handpiece, and the system automatically recognizes it via the DCS – Detection Control System. This makes Modula RF one of the most user-friendly and practical devices in its segment.

Most Reliable, Most Efficient, No Contraindications

Modula RF stands out as the most effective radiotherapy technology. With its powerful 300 Watts capacity, it quickly reaches 41°C for excellent and consistent results.

The Auto Temperature Control (ATC) system includes sensors in all handpieces, displaying real-time skin temperature. You can manually control the temperature or set automatic limits for worry-free operation. The ATC ensures patient safety and comfort throughout every treatment.
