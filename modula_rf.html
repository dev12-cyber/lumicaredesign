3-Step Rejuvenation Process to Restore Skin Tone & Elasticity

Monopolar

Monopolar technology delivers controlled thermal energy deep into the skin layers, reaching the dermis and subcutaneous tissue. This stimulates collagen production, tightens connective tissue, and improves skin firmness from within. Ideal for body treatments, it provides a noticeable lifting and contouring effect with long-lasting results.

Bipolar

Bipolar delivers energy between two electrodes, allowing for precise and controlled heating of the superficial skin layers. This targeted approach is perfect for delicate areas such as the face and neck, stimulating collagen production and enhancing skin firmness with minimal risk. Safe, gentle, and effective—bipolar RF is ideal for non-invasive facial rejuvenation and fine wrinkle reduction.

Quadripolar

Quadripolar RF technology uses four electrodes to deliver evenly distributed energy across larger treatment areas, ensuring deeper penetration with maximum control and no energy dispersion. This advanced system stimulates collagen production, improves skin elasticity, and effectively tightens and contours the body—offering visible results with enhanced comfort and safety.
