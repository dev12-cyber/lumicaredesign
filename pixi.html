PIXI & SAFETY GO HAND IN HAND:

1. Wireless charging eliminates the risk of accidents:

Pixi has a wireless charging system with lithium battery, which significantly reduces charging times. This eliminates the risk of accidents from contact with liquid substances as can happen with traditional connectors.

2. Ionization of gases:

PIXI works on the principle of ionization of gases present in the air. It generates a small electric arc to treat the dermal and epidermal areas selectively. This prevents irradiation or transferring excessive heat to the subcutaneous and surrounding areas. This ensures that the process & treatment is completely safe for the skin of the patient.

3. Meticulous calibration & selective action:

(Light as a pen, great in the hand)
PIXI is a light and handy instrument. It offers a constant emission of the output signal and a meticulous calibration. It does not cause any damage to the subcutaneous tissue. Its highly selective action causes the sublimation of only the most superficial layer of the skin, without involving the underlying tissues. This ensures that the skin of the patient is safe at all times.
